Contact Me

Want to get in touch? Whether you have a question, want to collaborate, or just want to say hi, I'd love to hear from you!

I created this page entirely on my own, carefully planning how each element would look and function. I started by setting up a simple navigation bar at the top so visitors can easily move between pages, and then added a bright, highlighted “Contact Me” heading to draw attention. I included a short introduction paragraph to let people know I’m open to questions or collaboration, and then embedded my Foxyform so anyone can send me a message directly. I also made the map float next to the form, making it easy for visitors to see my location while using the page. Along the way, I adjusted widths, floats, and spacing to make sure everything lines up neatly and looks professional. Overall, building this page taught me a lot about combining HTML, CSS, and embedded scripts to create a functional and visually appealing website.
